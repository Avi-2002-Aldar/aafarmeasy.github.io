PM Kisan Yojana Nidhi



Apply to Scheme:PM Kisan Yojana Nidhi
Objective:PM-KISAN is a government scheme in India aimed at providing direct income support to small and marginal farmers to assist in their agricultural expenses and livelihood.
Beneficiaries: The scheme targets farmers who own cultivable land up to 2 hectares, providing them with financial assistance.
Financial Assistance: Eligible farmers receive Rs. 6,000 per year, distributed in three equal installments of Rs. 2,000 each.
Payment Method: The financial aid is directly transferred to the beneficiaries' bank accounts through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system.
Application: Farmers can apply for the scheme through the PM-KISAN portal or their state government's designated portal by providing personal and land-related details.
Data Validation: Stringent validation processes are in place to prevent duplicate entries and ensure that only eligible beneficiaries receive the assistance.
Implementing Authority: The Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, along with state governments and union territories, is responsible for implementing the scheme.
Funding:Funding: The scheme is entirely funded by the Government of India. Updates: The scheme has been updated and expanded over time to enhance payment efficiency and data management.
