National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)


Apply to scheme: National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)
Objective: The primary goal of e-NAM is to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities, enabling transparent price discovery and efficient trading. Platform: e-NAM is a digital platform that provides a common marketplace for farmers, traders, and buyers to trade in agricultural commodities.
Features: The platform offers features such as real-time price information, online bidding, quality certification, and transparent transaction processes.
Benefits for Farmers: e-NAM helps farmers get access to a larger market and better prices for their produce. It reduces intermediaries and associated costs.
Market Integration: The platform integrates various physical markets (mandis) across states, creating a nationwide network of agricultural trading.
e-NAM Portal: The e-NAM platform has a user-friendly web portal where farmers can register, list their produce, and interact with buyers.
Auction Mechanism: The platform supports competitive bidding through an auction mechanism, ensuring fair prices for both farmers and buyers.
Quality Standards: e-NAM provides quality testing and certification services for commodities to ensure transparency and trust in transactions.
Payment and Settlement: The platform facilitates online payment and settlement, reducing delays and ensuring secure transactions.
Implementation: e-NAM is implemented by various state governments in collaboration with the central government and other stakeholders.
